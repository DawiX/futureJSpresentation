The business of front-end development
  • Learn core skills!!! HTML, CSS, Javascript

  • Others are just work flows that worked for someone before.

  • Frameworks can change, standards don't change

  • Don't invent a wheel

  • Accessibility should not be coming, it should be from start!
    • We don't stop playing because we get old, we get old because we stop playing.
